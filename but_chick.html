Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced garlic
  • 1/4 cup diced red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup cooked brown beans

    Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add the grated Gruyère cheese, onion, garlic, red pepper flakes, thyme, oregano, basil, rosemary, parsley, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper to the skillet.
  4. Cook for 5 more minutes, until the cheese is melted and the sauce is fragrant.
  5. Add the cooked brown rice and cook for 1 minute, or until the rice is tender.
  6. Add the cooked brown beans and cook for an additional 1-2
